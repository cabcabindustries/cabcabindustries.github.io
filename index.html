Fresh Vietnamese Limade

If you ever had pho, then you probably had a good chance of trying Vietnamese limade. The next time you go to a Vietnamese restaurant and if you like lemonade/limade, give it an order. There is nothing like burning your tongue on delicious pho broth and then trying to console your burnt tongue with ice cold limade. Created by heating up water, lime, and sugar until it becomes a sour syrup. After that, combine with water, sugar to your liking to sweeten the limade, and you 'll never want any other type of lemonade/limade again.
