Our Awesome Team

Meet Franky!

"Ca-CAW!" - Franky

Chrip! Chrip! Was that a bird? No it was Franky, our awesome bird watching expert who is able to imitate over 20 types of bird calls! Since a young child, he was always fascinated by birds. Spending most of his childhood hiking in the forests and mountains, he liked to imitate all the different birds he could hear. From a goldfinch to a red-tailed hawk, you wont be able to tell who is talking to who.

Say Hi to Candance

Did you know there are over 30,000 types of fish? Well, Candance is able to recognize over thousands of fish just by looking at them. Your kayak adventure will be fun and educational with Candance. However, if you're looking for more of a work-out, then she can also leave ya in the dust. For the past five years, guests are always amazed how much information is contained in her brain. No matter what question you have, Candance is always able to help.

"Be prepared for hot weather in Spring and Summer while fall and winter is a bit chilly. And don't forget your sunscreen! You can still get sunburnt in the snow!" -Candance

What's Up Greg?!

"Whether you're coming from a hot hike or kayaking in the cold ocean, there's nothing like pho to refill your energy or warm ya up nicely" -Greg

Don't feel like cooking? Head over to Pacific Coast Dining and enjoy home comfort food ranging from Asian to Mediterranean cuisine. Bored with his office cubicle life, Greg decided he wanted to share his love of food with people. And when he visited Pacific Coast Trails, he knew instantly where his next adventure would be. Now serving as our head chef, Greg has truly made Pacific Coast Trails feel like home with his ability to cook. If you can't decide on what to eat, Greg recommends the Pho soup.
