About Us

Meet Franky!

Did you hear that? Was it a plane? Was it a bird? No it was Franky, our awesome bird watching expert who is able to imitate over 20 types of birds! Since a young child, he was always fascinated by wildlife. Spending most of his childhood hiking in the forests and mountains, he liked to imitate all the different birds he could hear. From a red-tailed hawk to a goldfinch, you wont be able to tell who is talking to who.

About the trail

Birds, Bears, Waterfalls... Oh my! From beginner to advanced trails, there are plenty of trails to choose from depending on the hiking experience. we also have guided tours with some of the most knowledgeable guides you'll ever meet. If you plan on coming here in the fall and winter times, be prepared for cold and snow conditions. While the spring and summer are hot and sunny. Don't forget your sunscreen!
